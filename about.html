Meet Our Founder

Dubem Eneh, a Computer Science major with minors in Mathematics and Informatics, founded ShopEase with a mission to simplify shopping for everyone. His passion for innovation, problem-solving, and creating seamless online experiences has made ShopEase a trusted platform for customers worldwide.

As an aspiring Software Engineer, Dubem blends convenience, reliability, and modern technology to ensure that every aspect of ShopEase reflects his commitment to quality and efficiency. His vision continues to guide the company toward redefining the future of online shopping while leveraging his technical expertise to build impactful and user-friendly solutions.
